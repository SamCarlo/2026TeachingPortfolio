Section 2 Show It Skill: Showing

Pick one moment from your sequence above and write it as a scene. No summarizing, no stating emotions — show through action, dialogue, and sensory detail.

Before you move on, check:
  • I showed what my character does, not just what they feel
  • I included at least one sensory detail (sound, smell, touch, taste, or sight)
  • I included body language or a physical action
  • I did not summarize — every sentence is in-scene, not reported
